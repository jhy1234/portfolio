Biography

Huangyi Jiang is a front-end oriented developer who builds websites according to clients’ requirement. He has a solid understanding of web developing tools including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and PHP. He can also create websites using WordPress or create a custom theme. Huangyi will soon graduate from BCIT’s New Media Design and Web Development program, in which he studies design, UI/UX, video audio production as well as project management. This experience also makes him a team worker. He can cooperate with designers and other developers to make large projects.

Huangyi enjoys the process of creating things to help people succeed. He believes that it’s important to make the internet not only functional but also accessible to the audience. For this reason, he pays attention to unobvious details such as websites’ accessibility, readability and responsiveness. He is also highly motivated in learning new technologies such as the React library.

Huangyi has a BA in foreign language and culture from Beijing Foreign Studies University. He served three years as a photographer for the university’s newspaper. In 2008, he volunteered for the Beijing Olympic Games. He then studied history at Simon Fraser University. There he was inspired by the dramatic changing of the online world and decided to join in this trend of creating.
